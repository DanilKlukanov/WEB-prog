Hotline Miami

Rules

Welcome to the game! There are some rules here:
1) You have 5 lives. If you lose them all, you will end.
2) There are 4 types of enemies in the game. Each has its own speed and the number of points for the destruction.
3) You can apply “over kick” for 500 points. All enemies will be destroyed. Double click on a key.
4) There are 3 waves, you can reach 3, then you are cool!.
